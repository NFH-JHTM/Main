Đỗ Gia Hân

💖 Thông tin cá nhân:

  • 📅 Ngày Sinh: 14/04/2007
  • 📍 Quê Quán: Phú Yên
  • Thường dân tổ 3

With your glittering eyes, need to light;
Your hair is so tender that I desire no spring.
In this world I see no fair lady,
So I call you a Beauty: Gia Han

And

Every time when you open your velvet-soft eyes, they seem to pull my whole soul into a world of love, shaking with a tremulous ecstasy.
